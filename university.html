EXAMPLES AT UNIVERSITY

Here are a couple of mock websites that were created for university coursework.

SALTWAY COUNCIL [2011]

Using the ficticious scenario below, the project was to build a website as a group, in order to test our web programming skills.

"You have successfully bid on a project to develop a website with a messaging component for Saltway Council, a small local council that is responsible for an urban borough, home to a population of around 60,000."

Baltika Beer [2012]

This project's aim was to test team working and presentation skills. The task was to pitch a product from a given list, creating either a website or a brochure to convince a retailer to stock the product. Our group decided on a russian alcoholic beverage that is not sold in the country.
